Bill Self

Bill's Bio:

Bill Eugene Self Jr is the head coach of the University of Kansas men's basketball team. In his 13 seasons as head coach at KU, he has won 12 straight Big 12 regular season titles and in 2008 won the NCAA championship. At Allen Fieldhouse, his teams have only suffered a total of 9 losses. That means he has won more Big 12 Championships than he has lost games at Allen Fieldhouse.
